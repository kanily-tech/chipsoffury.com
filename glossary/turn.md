---
term: Turn
---

The fourth community card, dealt face-up after the flop betting round ends.

After the turn is dealt, there's another betting round. Same as the flop - first to act is the player left of the dealer button.

**Why "turn"?** It's when the hand often "turns" - a card that changes everything. A draw completes, a pair becomes trips, or a scare card shifts who's ahead. You'll hear players say "the turn changed everything" when this happens.

**Also called:** Fourth street (from older poker variants).
