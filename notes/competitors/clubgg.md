## ClubGG

### 1. Quick Facts
- **Marketing URL**: https://www.clubgg.com
- **Developer**: NSUS Limited (parent company of GGPoker), launched January 2021
- **Platforms Supported**: iOS, Android, Windows, and Mac (native clients for all platforms; no web browser support)
- **System Requirements**: iOS 14+ or Android 9+, approximately 400 MB free space
- **Platform Type**: Mobile-first social poker app with desktop support, focused on private clubs and WSOP/live tournament satellites
- **Best For**: Private poker clubs, communities wanting structured tournaments, and players seeking WSOP satellite access
- **Cost to Host**: Free for clubs up to 100 members (Level 0); premium club levels require monthly fees but are significantly cheaper than competing apps (50-80% cost reduction)
- **Player Membership Options**: Free (play chips only), Standard ($9.99/month), Platinum ($49.99/month) for tournament access
- **Registration Required**: Yes (email or social login, account verified via email code, 18+ age requirement, country assigned at registration)

### 2. Core Features
- **Private Club System**: Create or join clubs with customizable roles, membership management, union/alliance support, and up to 100 free members per club
- **Game Formats**: Texas Hold'em, Omaha, 5 Card Omaha, tournaments with multi-table support (up to 4 tables simultaneously)
- **Customization Options**: All-In Insurance, Running it Twice, IP/GPS/Device restrictions, adjustable game length, and chat controls
- **GGClass Training**: Built-in poker strategy videos and training platform (included with Standard and Platinum memberships)
- **WSOP Satellites**: Access to World Series of Poker Main Event packages and other live tournament satellites (APT, RGPS)
- **Social Features**: Friends lists, in-game chat/emojis, community feed highlighting big winners, and social interaction tools
- **Stats & Tools**: PokerCraft, Smart HUD, game history tracking, Rabbit Hunt, Splash, River Squeeze features
- **Live Dollars (L$) System**: Virtual currency for tournament entry (Note: Direct cash redemptions suspended as of April 2025; prizes now tournament tickets and live event packages)

### 3. Pricing & Monetization

**For Players:**
- **Free Membership**: Download and play with virtual Play Chips in clubs, no monetary value. Can create or join clubs and play for fun
- **Standard Membership ($9.99/month)**: Access to tournaments with Live Dollar prizes, 500 starting chips bonus, PokerCraft, Social Casino, Smart HUD, Rabbit Hunt, Splash, River Squeeze, GGClass training, limited to one daily Stage 1 tournament attempt
- **Platinum Membership ($49.99/month)**: All Standard features plus 2,500 starting chips bonus, unlimited Stage 1 access, Final Stage re-entry, loyalty bonus, opportunity to win share of $200,000 monthly in tournament dollars, access to 100+ WSOP Main Event packages via satellites

**For Club Organizers:**
- **Level 0 (Free)**: Up to 100 members, basic club features
- **Premium Club Levels**: Monthly subscription fees required for clubs exceeding 100 members, unlocking higher member caps and additional tables/features. Costs are 50-80% lower than competing poker apps
- **Operational Costs**: Free to add chips and operate tables (unlike most apps that charge high fees for chips and table operations)

**Important 2025 Update:**
- As of April 2025, direct cash redemptions for Live Dollars have been suspended
- Platform shifted from sweepstakes model to subscription-based poker app
- Rewards now primarily tournament tickets and live event packages (WSOP seats, etc.) rather than cash payouts
- UK market access suspended in March 2025 pending regulatory discussions

### 4. Pros
- **Comprehensive Platform Support**: Native apps for iOS, Android, Windows, and Mac (no emulators needed)
- **Cost-Effective for Organizers**: 50-80% cheaper than competing apps, with free chip distribution and table operations
- **Robust Club Features**: Advanced membership management, union/alliance system, customizable tables, and role-based permissions
- **WSOP Access**: Unique satellite tournaments offering 100+ WSOP Main Event packages and other major live tournament entries
- **Professional-Grade Client**: GGPoker-powered technology with modern UI, multi-table support (4 tables), and comprehensive stats/HUD
- **Training Resources**: Integrated GGClass platform with poker strategy videos included in paid memberships
- **Social Community**: Strong social features including clubs, friends lists, chat, and community feeds
- **Flexible Customization**: Extensive game settings including All-In Insurance, Running it Twice, and various restriction options

### 5. Cons
- **No Web Browser Support**: Requires downloading native apps; cannot play directly in a web browser
- **Complex Setup for Organizers**: Club Level tiers, parameters, and limits can be confusing for new organizers
- **Mobile-First Design**: Some users find the portrait layout and mobile-centric interface less ideal for traditional desktop play
- **No Cash Redemptions**: As of April 2025, players cannot cash out Live Dollars for money; rewards are tournament tickets and live event packages only
- **Regional Restrictions**: UK access suspended as of March 2025; country assigned at registration cannot be changed
- **Subscription Required for Tournaments**: Free users limited to play chips; must pay $9.99-$49.99/month for competitive play and prizes
- **Platform in Transition**: Recent major policy changes (cash redemption suspension, UK exit, social casino shutdown) indicate ongoing strategic shifts

### 6. Security & Fairness
- **Developer Credibility**: Built by NSUS Limited, the parent company of GGPoker (one of the world's largest poker networks)
- **Data Protection**: Reduced banking data exposure since April 2025 cash redemption suspension
- **Game Integrity**: Anti-cheating security functions, IP/GPS/Device restrictions available for club organizers
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Clear positioning as social poker app; media guidelines emphasize separation from real-money gambling
- **Account Security**: Email verification required, unique Account ID generation, standard terms of service and privacy policy
- **Age Verification**: 18+ requirement enforced (19 in Alabama, 21 in Massachusetts)
- **Club Governance**: Platform-level policies for acceptable use, organizer controls for membership management

### 7. Best Suited For
- **Poker Clubs and Communities**: Groups wanting structured, recurring private games with professional-grade features
- **WSOP Aspirants**: Players seeking affordable satellite access to World Series of Poker and other major live tournaments
- **Tournament Organizers**: Club managers running leagues, series, and scheduled events with multiple tables
- **Mobile-First Players**: Users who prefer mobile poker with optional desktop support
- **Budget-Conscious Organizers**: Club hosts looking for cost-effective platform (50-80% cheaper than alternatives)
- **Cross-Platform Groups**: Communities with members on iOS, Android, Windows, and Mac devices
- **Learning Players**: Those who value integrated training resources (GGClass) alongside gameplay
- **Social Poker Enthusiasts**: Players prioritizing community features, friends lists, and social interaction over cash games

### 8. Quick Comparison Notes
- **vs. Browser-Only Tools (Poker Now)**: ClubGG offers richer native clients, professional GGPoker technology, and advanced club tiers, but requires app downloads and has more setup complexity
- **vs. Lightweight Friend Apps**: ClubGG feels like a full-featured platform with structured clubs and alliances rather than casual friend games
- **vs. Real-Money Poker (GGPoker)**: ClubGG focuses on social play, club management, and WSOP satellites rather than cash games; uses Play Chips and Live Dollars instead of real money
- **vs. Competing Club Apps (PokerBros, Pokerrrr 2)**: ClubGG offers 50-80% cost savings for organizers, broader platform support (including Mac), and integrated WSOP satellite access
- **Unique Positioning**: Bridges social poker and live tournament access; shifted from sweepstakes model to subscription-based app as of 2025
