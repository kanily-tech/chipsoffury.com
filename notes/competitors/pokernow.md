## Poker Now

### 1. Quick Facts
- **Marketing URL**: https://www.pokernow.club[1]
- **Platforms Supported**: Web browser only (works on any device with modern browser - desktop, mobile, tablet); compatible with Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, Android via browser; no native apps; no install required[2][3]
- **Platform Type**: Browser-based online home-game platform with optional clubs layer[1][2]
- **Best For**: Groups that want instant, link-based tables and/or lightweight clubs directly in the browser[2]
- **Cost to Host**: Core tables free; "Poker Now Plus" ($9.99/month or $69.60/year) and "Platinum" ($49.99/month or $499/year) subscriptions unlock premium features including Clubs[4][5]
- **Registration Required**: No registration or login required for basic gameplay; accounts required only for Plus/Platinum subscriptions and Clubs feature[6][7]
- **Developer**: Created by Brazilian developer Samuel Sim√µes; now operated by Rio Gaming, Inc. (partnership formed 2024-2025)[8]

### 2. Core Features
- Instant private tables via shareable links with in-browser UI, built-in video/voice chat, balance tracking, and hand histories[2][9]
- "Poker Now Clubs" (for Plus/Platinum members) allow organizers to create persistent lobbies with membership lists, multiple tables, announcements, credit/balance tracking, and live ledger[4][10]
- Built-in video and voice chat (no third-party apps needed)[2][9]
- Supports multiple poker variants including Texas Hold'em, PLO, Omaha, Seven-Card Stud[11]
- Up to 10 players per table for cash games[12]
- Multi-table tournaments (MTT) support with late registration for Plus members[13]
- AI-powered game analysis "Sam Says..." provides insights, nicknames, and grades for players (Plus/Platinum feature)[5][13]

### 3. Pricing & Monetization
- **Free Tier**: Core Poker Now service is completely free with no registration required[2][6]
- **Poker Now Plus**: $9.99/month or $69.60/year (save $50 on annual plan)[4]
  - 7-day trial period
  - Features: Unlimited video/chat, custom avatars, custom felt designs, game modifications (7-2 Bounty, Double Boards), unlimited hand histories, enhanced hand replayer with filtering tools, AI-powered game analysis, priority tournament notifications, late registration for MTTs (up to 30 minutes), 2 free clubs with up to 10 members each during beta[4][5][10][13]
- **Poker Now Platinum**: $49.99/month or $499/year (save $100 on annual plan)[4]
  - All Plus features
  - 10 clubs with 100 players per club
  - 600 players multi-table tournament capability
  - Additional premium features[4]

### 4. Pros
- Entirely browser-based, so players can join from almost any modern device without installing apps; works on Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, Android[2][3]
- No download or registration required for basic free gameplay - just enter a nickname and share a room link[6][7]
- Built-in video and voice chat eliminates need for third-party communication apps[2][9]
- Completely free for casual play with no real-money gambling (play money only)[2][14]
- Clubs offer a lightweight way to run ongoing private communities with multiple tables, balance history, and member management[4][10]
- Large buttons, clear fonts, and user-friendly interface[12]
- Free tournaments available with real prizes (Amazon Gift Cards)[15]
- AI-powered analytics and game insights for premium members[5][13]

### 5. Cons
- Clubs feature is in beta and initially limited (2 clubs with 10 members each for Plus tier during trial)[10]
- Graphics are functional but simpler than native mobile apps that prioritize animations and visual effects[2]
- No native mobile apps - relies entirely on browser compatibility[3]
- Maximum 10 players per cash game table (smaller than some competitors)[12]
- Premium features require paid subscription[4][5]
- No real-money gaming (play money only)[14]

### 6. Security & Fairness
- Uses secure web connections (HTTPS) and modern web security standards with cookie-based sessions[2]
- Provides public information about platform operation and community rules through blog and terms pages[1]
- Since games are browser-based and play-money only, security relies on group composition for trust in private games[2]
- Platform acquired by Rio Gaming, Inc. providing additional corporate backing and resources[8]

### 7. Best Suited For
- Casual players wanting zero-install, browser-only home games where everyone can join via a link[1][2][6]
- Friend groups and home game organizers who prefer simplicity over complex features[2][7]
- Organizers who want to manage small clubs directly in the browser rather than through a dedicated app[4][10]
- Players who want built-in video/voice chat without setting up third-party apps[2][9]
- Groups looking for free, play-money poker without real-money gambling[2][14]

### 8. Recent Updates & Changes
- Partnership with Rio Gaming, Inc. formed in 2024-2025 to provide resources for platform growth[8]
- Launch of Poker Now Plus and Platinum subscription tiers with AI-powered analytics[4][5][13]
- Introduction of Poker Now Clubs beta feature (exclusive to paid members)[10]
- Free tournaments launched with real prizes (Amazon Gift Cards)[15]
- Enhanced hand replayer with filtering tools added for Plus members[5][13]
- Game modifications introduced: 7-2 Bounty and Double Boards (Plus feature)[5][13]

### 9. Quick Comparison Notes
- Compared to ClubGG and PPPoker, Poker Now is far simpler to access (browser-only, no registration), at the cost of fewer native-client features and cosmetics[2]
- Compared to Unmasked Poker, Poker Now now has built-in video/voice chat as a core feature (previous gap has been closed)[2][9]
- Poker Now focuses on play-money only while competitors may offer real-money gaming options[14]
