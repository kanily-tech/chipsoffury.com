## EasyPoker

### 1. Quick Facts
- **Marketing URL**: https://easy.poker[1]
- **Platforms Supported**: iOS and Android (native apps)[2][3]. No native desktop or web browser versions; desktop play possible only via Android emulators like BlueStacks[4]
- **Platform Type**: Mobile-first app designed for simple "poker with friends" games[1][5]
- **Best For**: Casual mobile groups that want quick, simple private games without complex club structures or real-money gambling[1][6]
- **Cost to Host**: Free to download and play (up to 6 players with free version). Optional EasyPoker Plus subscription for advanced features and larger tables (up to 12 players)[7][8]
- **Registration Required**: No account registration required to play. Users can create or join games instantly using a simple 4-digit game PIN[9][10]
- **User Base**: Over 1.5 million players (as of 2025)[11]

### 2. Core Features
- **Game Variants**: Texas Hold'em, Omaha, Short Deck (Six Plus), and Reversed Hold'em[12][13]
- **Table Size**: 2-12 players. Free tier supports up to 6 players; EasyPoker Plus subscription required for tables of 7-12 players[14][15]
- **One-Tap Setup**: Create private games instantly with a 4-digit PIN code; no complex setup required[10][16]
- **Real-Time Voice Calls**: Built-in voice communication during gameplay for seamless interaction[17][18]
- **Customization**: Adjust rules, blinds, buy-ins, rebuys, and table settings; custom playing cards and themes available[19][20]
- **Analytics & Stats**: Advanced metrics including Aggression Factor, Win Percentage, PFR, VPIP, ATS, WTSD for tracking performance[21][22]
- **Poker Passport**: Built-in learning tool that tracks moves and provides insights for skill improvement[23]
- **Emotes & Messages**: Dynamic reactions and pre-crafted messages for engaging with other players[24]
- **Leaderboard System**: Rank progression with rewards and recognition[25]
- **Random Matchmaking**: Public games available for Texas Hold'em and Omaha[26]
- **Mobile-Optimized**: Vertical interface design specifically for one-handed smartphone play[27]

### 3. Pricing & Monetization
- **Business Model**: Subscription-based (first poker app to run entirely on subscriptions; does not facilitate real-money gambling)[28][29]
- **Free Tier**: Unlimited play with up to 6 players, includes basic features like voice calls, multiple game variants, and Poker Passport[30][31]
- **EasyPoker Plus Subscription Pricing**[32][33]:
  - Weekly: $3.99
  - Monthly: $5.99
  - 6 Months: $29.99
  - Annual: $29.99 (promotional pricing; regular price $39.99)
- **Plus Benefits**: Tables up to 12 players, enhanced customization options, rebuy functionality, custom table settings, advanced analytics, ad-free experience, additional game types, and premium themes[34][35]
- **Important Note**: Only the host needs EasyPoker Plus; free players can join Plus tables without restrictions[36]

### 4. Pros
- **No Registration Required**: Instant play with no sign-up; true privacy-first approach using simple 4-digit PIN codes[37][38]
- **User-Friendly Design**: Clean, modern interface designed for casual and new players; simple one-handed vertical gameplay[39][40]
- **Complete Free Tier**: Genuinely free unlimited poker for up to 6 players with core features included (unlike many "freemium" apps)[41][42]
- **Built-in Voice Calls**: No need for external communication tools; seamless voice chat integrated directly[43]
- **No Real Money Gambling**: Focus on social play without the risks or legal complexities of real-money poker[44]
- **Cross-Platform Play**: iOS and Android users can play together at the same tables[45]
- **Quick Setup**: One-tap game creation; faster to start than dealing physical cards[46]
- **Regular Updates**: Active development with consistent bug fixes, feature additions, and stability improvements[47][48]

### 5. Cons
- **Mobile-Only Platform**: No native desktop or web browser versions; desktop play requires Android emulators which can be cumbersome[49][50]
- **Limited Club Features**: Less suitable for complex club management, tournament series, or large poker communities compared to PPPoker/Pokerrrr 2[51]
- **Player Limit on Free Tier**: Free version caps tables at 6 players; need subscription for 7-12 players[52]
- **Subscription Required for Full Features**: Advanced customization, rebuys, and larger tables locked behind paywall[53]
- **No Tablet Optimization**: Designed for vertical smartphone play; may not offer optimal experience on tablets or landscape mode[54]

### 6. Security & Fairness
- **App Store Security**: Distributed through official Apple App Store and Google Play Store with standard security review processes[55][56]
- **Privacy-First Approach**: No mandatory registration or account creation; minimal personal data collection[57]
- **Social Trust Model**: As a friends-only social poker app (no real money), fairness relies on playing with known, trusted groups[58]
- **Transparent Settings**: All table rules, blinds, and game settings are visible to all players before joining[59]
- **No Gambling Risks**: Since no real money is involved, there are no financial security concerns or gambling-related risks[60]

### 7. Best Suited For
- **Casual Friend Groups**: Small circles of friends (2-6 players) looking for simple, no-setup poker nights[61][62]
- **Mobile-First Players**: Users who primarily play on smartphones and prefer vertical/one-handed gameplay[63]
- **Privacy-Conscious Users**: Players who want to avoid account registration and minimize data sharing[64]
- **Poker Beginners**: New players who appreciate the simple interface, in-app tutorial, and Poker Passport learning features[65]
- **Social Players**: Groups that value built-in voice chat and emote interactions during gameplay[66]
- **Non-Gambling Groups**: Family, coworkers, or social circles who want poker fun without real-money stakes[67]
- **Subscription-Tolerant Hosts**: Users willing to pay $30-40/year for premium features and larger tables[68]

### 8. Quick Comparison Notes
- **vs. Poker Now/Unmasked**: EasyPoker is mobile-app focused (no web version) while competitors offer browser-based play; better mobile UX but less platform flexibility[69][70]
- **vs. PPPoker/Pokerrrr 2**: Targets smaller friend groups (2-12 players) rather than large club networks; simpler interface but fewer club management tools[71][72]
- **vs. Real Money Poker Apps**: EasyPoker is purely social (no gambling), making it legal and accessible everywhere without age restrictions or financial risks[73]
- **Unique Differentiators**: Only major poker app with no mandatory registration; first to run entirely on subscription model (no ads, no gambling)[74][75]

### 9. Recent Updates & Development (2024-2025)
- **Version 1.3.5** (Latest): Minor bug fixes and general stability improvements[76]
- **Recent Feature Additions**[77][78]:
  - Emotes & pre-crafted messages for enhanced player interaction
  - Random matchmaking for Omaha (in addition to Texas Hold'em)
  - Leaderboard enhancements with scroll-to-top functionality
  - New chip styles unlocked by leveling up
  - In-app tutorial for first-time users
  - Improved profile picture management
  - Fixed voice call bugs and stuck emote issues
  - Enhanced showdown card display when players are all-in
- **Development Status**: Actively maintained with regular updates; reached 1.5M+ user milestone[79][80]
