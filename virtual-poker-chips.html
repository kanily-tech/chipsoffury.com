Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about using digital poker chips for home games

What exactly is Chips Only Mode?

Chips Only Mode is our unique feature that turns your device into a poker chip counter app while you play with physical cards. It manages all the digital aspects of poker (betting, pot calculation, chip management) while you enjoy the tactile feel of real cards. Think of it as having a professional dealer managing the chips, while you focus on the cards and strategy.

Do I need physical poker chips at all?

No! That's the beauty of it. Our virtual chips for live poker completely replace physical chips. All you need is a deck of cards and each player's phone. The app handles all chip tracking, making it perfect for impromptu games or when you've forgotten your chip set.

How does betting work without chips?

Players use their phone screen to make bets. You'll see your chip count and can tap to check, bet, raise, or fold. The interface shows bet amounts, pot size, and required calls - just like a real table. Our poker chip tracking app ensures every bet is recorded accurately and the pot is always correct.

What happens at showdown?

Players reveal their physical cards as normal. Then, simply tap on the winner(s) in the app. The pot is automatically distributed, including proper split pot calculations. Side pots are handled automatically too - no complex math required!

Can we play different poker variations?

Absolutely! Chips Only Mode works with any poker variation - Texas Hold'em, Omaha, Stud, Draw games, and more. Since we don't deal cards, you can play any game you know the rules for. The app handles the betting and chips for all variations.

Is it really free? Any hidden costs?

Yes, it's completely free! No ads, no premium features, no hidden costs. We believe poker with friends should be accessible to everyone. You can play unlimited games with unlimited players at no cost.

What if someone's phone dies mid-game?

No problem! The game state is saved across all devices. The player can rejoin on another device, or you can manage their stack for them. The game continues smoothly without losing any data.
