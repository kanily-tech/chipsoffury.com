Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about using digital poker chips for home games

What exactly is Chips Only Mode?

Chips Only Mode turns Chips of Fury into a digital dealer for your live game. You bring the cards, and we automate chip counts, betting rounds, and pot math so you can focus on the action.

How do we handle betting without physical chips?

Each player uses their phone to check, bet, raise, or fold. The app tracks chip stacks, blinds, antes, and side pots automatically—no counting required. At showdown you simply tap the winner(s) and the app distributes the pot with perfect math.

Can we play custom games like Teen Patti?

Yes! Freestyle mode lets you run any card game with digital chips—Teen Patti, in-house casino games, or your own creations. For standard poker variants (Hold'em, Omaha, Stud, and more), enable "Poker Mode" betting rules for structured blinds and rounds.

Is Chips Only Mode free?

Yes, Chips Only Mode is 100% free with no ads. This includes both Freestyle chip tracking and full Poker mode with guided betting rounds and blind structures. No account required to start playing.

Does it need an internet connection?

Yes - the app syncs everyone's phones in real time, so you need some kind of internet connection. Wi-Fi, cellular data, or a mobile hotspot all work fine. Playing at a campsite or cabin with no Wi-Fi? One person can share a phone hotspot and everyone else connects to that. Uses very little data.

What if someone's phone dies mid-game?

Create a free Chips of Fury account before you start. If your device dies, you can log in on another device and pick up right where you left off. Guest sessions stay on the original device and can’t be transferred.
