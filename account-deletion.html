Account Deletion - Chips of Fury

Last updated: September 26, 2025

Chips of Fury is published by Kanily Technologies LLP. This page explains how you can ask us to delete your Chips of Fury player account and the data connected to it.

Request account deletion

Follow the steps to delete your account:

  1. Login with the account that you want to delete
  2. Go to the accounts page (top left menu button on the home page)
  3. Click on the Delete Account button (at the bottom of the account page)
  4. Confirm Deletion

Data that will be deleted

Following data is immediately deleted

  • Account profile information (display name, email address, avatar, linked social accounts).

Data we retain

  • Game history of games where you have already played, as these games are shared with other players
  • Your nickname is still visible in these old game snapshots
