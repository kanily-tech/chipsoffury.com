Delete Your Chips of Fury Account

Chips of Fury is published by Kanily Technologies LLP. Follow the steps below if you would like to permanently delete your account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Sign in with the account you want to delete.
  2. Open the menu and tap Account.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and choose Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What gets deleted immediately

Once you confirm deletion, we remove:

  • Profile information (display name, email address, avatar, linked social accounts).
  • Any private notes or device-specific preferences.

What we retain

Completed game snapshots are shared with every player at the table, so we keep limited historical records for fairness:

  • Past game history where you already played.
  • Your nickname as it appears inside those archived games.

We never use retained records for marketing and we do not sell your data.

Need help?

If you can’t access your account or need help with the process, contact us and select the “Account & Privacy” topic. We’ll verify ownership before processing the request manually.
